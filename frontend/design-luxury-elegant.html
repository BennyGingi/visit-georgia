02

Wine & Gastronomy

The World's
Oldest Wine Country

Eight thousand years before the great Bordeaux chateaux existed, Georgians were fermenting grapes in clay qvevri buried in the earth. Kakheti's amber wines carry the memory of civilizations. Taste them where they were born, in the family estates of the Alazani Valley.

  • Kakheti full-day wine tours
  • Private cellar tastings & qvevri visits
  • Traditional Georgian feast (Supra)
  • Harvest season experiences (September)

03

Tbilisi & Culture

A City of
Beautiful Contrasts

Founded in the 5th century, Tbilisi is a living museum where Persian and Soviet architecture share cobblestone alleys with contemporary galleries. The Abanotubani sulfur bath district, Narikala Fortress, and the Dry Bridge market form a city unlike any other in the Caucasus.

  • Tbilisi private city walking tours
  • Mtskheta UNESCO World Heritage visit
  • Abanotubani sulfur bath experience
  • Airport pickup & city transfers
